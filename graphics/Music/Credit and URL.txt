How to Attribute

When using our music, you must include correct attribution in your video description or on your website. This consists of writing both the artist name(s) and track title(s) of the music you've used and including a clickable link to Free To Use (https://freetouse.com/music). Each time you download a track, a little pop-up will appear showcasing an example of correct attribution. This can conveniently be copied and pasted into your video descriptions, ensuring compliance with our usage policy. If you have received a copyright claim on your video, please visit https://freetouse.com/music/received-a-copyright-claim for more information.
